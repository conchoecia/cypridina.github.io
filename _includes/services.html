The Project

We aim to sequence luminous organisms spanning the tree of life.

Sequencing

De novo genomic sequencing was prohibitively expensive until very recently. Our team will use a proprietary library construction method to produce luminous organism genomes for approximately $1,000 (200Mbp).

Motivation

The generated genomes will provide valuable insights into the evolution of bioluminescence and provide new technologies to advance biomedical science and energy-free civilian lighting sources.
